## Full Description (≤4000 chars)

**A quiet life, yours to build.**

Pixel Town is a cozy life simulation where you move to a small town, pick up a job, tend a garden, and make friends with the neighbors. There's no combat, no rush, no timer counting down. Just a town at a comfortable pace, with four seasons and a cast of characters who remember what you did last spring.

**Live, work, and belong.**

Choose from four career paths — Service, Skilled Trade, Self-Employed, or Creative — and watch your character's savings and reputation grow over time. Farm a plot of land east of town or cast a line down at the beach. Cook meals from your harvest and bring them over to the librarian you've been meaning to ask out. There's a wedding hall on the edge of town, and it's been empty a while.

**The twist no other cozy game has.**

You will age. One day your character will retire, and when they do, their adult child picks up where they left off — same town, same friendships, different choices. Your fishing rod, your farm, your house: all of it passes to the next generation. Play long enough and you'll watch great-grandchildren tend the same vegetable patch you planted in year one.

**Features:**
- Cozy pixel-art life sim — top-down 16×16 tile world
- Four career paths: Service, Skilled Trade, Self-Employed, Creative
- Farm, fish, cook, forage, craft
- Date, marry, start a family
- Four zones to explore: Town, Farm, Forest, Beach
- Day/night cycle and four seasons
- Generational saves — your story continues through your kids
- No ads, no energy timers, no pay-to-win

*Plant, love, pass it on.*
